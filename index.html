About

I'm a senior at Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology (DA-IICT) pursuing my B. Tech. in Information and Communication Technology (ICT). My research interests lie at the intersection of Computer Science and Biology, and I use some basic techniques of mathematics in my research.

I've just finished two fantastic research projects DNACloud and ImPatho which were presented, by me, at International Conferences. I'm an undergraduate researcher at Laboratory of Natural Information Processing at DA-IICT, working on my next research project on DNA Tile Self-Assembly. I'm also awarded the position of Teaching Assistant at DA-IICT for my excellent academic performance. I'm primarily advised by Dr. Manish Gupta and Dr. Anil K Roy.

To know more about me, please have a look at my CV in printable document format here.
